问题 0812: Research Procedures
--------------------------------
复制以下全部内容：

Data will be collected online via platforms such as Prolific and the IU Psychology Student Pool (SONA). The study procedures are identical for both subject groups.

Duration: The study consists of a single session lasting approximately 20 minutes.

Procedures:

Participants will complete the study entirely online at a location of their choosing. After accessing the study via a secure link and reviewing the Study Information Sheet, clicking "Continue" indicates consent to participate.

The experiment includes two main phases:

1. Individual Phase:
Participants will view a series of stimuli (such as words or images) and later complete a memory test where they indicate whether each item is "OLD" (previously seen) or "NEW" (not seen before). Participants may also rate their confidence in their judgments. This phase is completed independently with no interaction with other participants.

2. Collaborative Phase:
Participants will be grouped with other participants online. During this phase, participants can see information about other group members' responses (such as their judgments and/or confidence ratings), view summary statistics about the group's answers, and optionally discuss items through a text-based interface. Participants can then revise their original answers if they choose. All interaction occurs online through the web interface with no real-time video or audio communication.

At the end, participants answer a brief question about whether they used external aids and receive a completion code for compensation.
